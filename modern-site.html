InSky Legal™

Professional UAV platform engineered to deliver maximum capability within the FAA Part 107 regulatory category.

The Legal platform allows operators to perform professional aerial work while remaining within the 55 lb (25 kg) maximum takeoff weight limit that governs most commercial drone operations.

Key Specifications

  • 14.4 kg ready-to-fly weight without payload
  • Up to 10.5 kg payload capacity while remaining within the 55 lb / 25 kg regulatory limit
  • Designed for professional aerial imaging, inspection, and sensor deployment
  • Rapid deployment architecture for field operations
  • Compatible with battery, hybrid, or tethered power configurations

This combination of low structural weight and useful payload capacity allows operators to deploy professional payload systems while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Professional Payload Ecosystem

The Legal platform integrates easily with professional imaging and sensor payloads through a modular payload mounting architecture compatible with widely used cinematography accessory standards.

The aircraft supports:

  • 12 mm dual-rail accessory systems commonly used in professional camera rigs
  • Toad-style quick-release mounting systems used in aerial cinematography workflows

This architecture allows rapid integration of professional payload systems including:

  • Stabilized camera gimbals
  • LiDAR and photogrammetry sensors
  • Broadcast and cinematography cameras
  • Inspection and monitoring equipment
  • Lighting systems
  • Specialized mission payloads

Because these mounting systems are widely used throughout the professional cinematography industry, operators can access a large ecosystem of high-end accessories and payload components already available on the market.

This approach allows users to deploy proven payload equipment without being restricted to proprietary mounting solutions.

References to mounting interfaces commonly used within the professional cinematography ecosystem are provided solely to describe mechanical compatibility with widely used accessory standards. InSky Aerospace is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with any third-party manufacturers whose products may be compatible with these interfaces.

InSky Hevi™

Heavy-lift aerial platform designed for industrial operations and large-payload aerial work.

The Hevi platform expands the capabilities of unmanned aircraft into applications that traditionally required helicopters or ground-based lifting equipment.

Key Capabilities

  • Up to 250 kg payload capacity
  • Modular airframe architecture supporting multiple mission configurations
  • Compatible with battery, hybrid, or tethered power systems
  • Designed for demanding operational environments

Hevi aircraft support a wide range of industrial and operational missions including:

  • Heavy equipment transport
  • Infrastructure construction support
  • Utility and powerline operations
  • Industrial logistics
  • Emergency response and disaster support
  • Specialized aerial work

By combining large payload capacity with modular payload integration, the Hevi platform enables aerial solutions that were previously difficult or impractical using conventional UAV systems.

Modular Power Systems

InSky aircraft are designed with flexible power architectures that allow operators to optimize performance for different operational requirements.

Battery Operation

For quiet, mobile operations and rapid deployment missions.

Hybrid Power Systems

For extended endurance missions requiring long flight durations.

Tethered Power Operation

For persistent aerial tasks such as communications relay, lighting, monitoring, and surveillance.

This flexibility allows operators to configure aircraft performance based on mission duration, payload weight, and operational constraints.
