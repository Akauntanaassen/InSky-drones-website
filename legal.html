InSky Legal™

A compact high-capability professional UAV platform

Designed for payload, inspection, imaging, and specialized field operations.

Legal is InSky’s lead near-term commercial platform. It is designed to combine low structural weight, strong useful payload capability, and a broad mission envelope in a compact professional aircraft.

PayloadInspectionImagingProfessional workflows
Why it matters

More capability without moving to a generic heavy platform

Many professional UAV platforms force a compromise between compact size, useful payload, and mission flexibility. Legal is being developed to reduce that compromise and support more capable field operations in a compact platform.

It is intended for operators who need a compact aircraft that can carry meaningful payload, support serious field workflows, and adapt to multiple mission profiles without being locked into a narrow equipment stack.

Operating framework

Capability beyond the standard threshold

Legal is designed with capability beyond the standard 55 lb U.S. small-UAS operating threshold.

In the United States, operations above that threshold require the appropriate regulatory exemption, including 44807 where applicable.

Payload and integration

Designed for professional workflows

Legal is designed as a professional platform with meaningful useful payload, compact deployment, modular mission integration, and compatibility with established professional accessory workflows.

Depending on mission requirements, the platform direction includes battery, hybrid, and tethered power approaches.

Commercial direction

Built for serious market validation

Legal’s early commercial path is centered on test validation, showroom and demo readiness, distributor and mission-fit conversations, and the conversion of serious interest into reservations.
