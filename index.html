Legal platform

Competition-level agility in the sub‑55 lb class.

Legal is tuned to contest the Freefly AltaX by delivering near double the performance while staying within the FAA Part 107 friendly weight envelope that end users expect for their tactical missions. It accepts the same 12 mm rail accessories and Freefly's Toad-in-the-Hole connector so customers can plug into their existing payload suites.

(Need a waiver? Legal also supports up to 44 kg MTOW when you want maximum endurance instead of regulatory minimums.)
