InSky Span™

Advanced heavy-lift aerial platform in development

Developed for industrial payload transport, rugged-terrain logistics, emergency support, and other large-payload field missions.

Span represents InSky’s larger-platform direction for missions where helicopters, cranes, or difficult ground transport are often the only alternatives.

Discuss a mission

What it is built to solve

Heavy-lift aircraft become valuable when they reduce the cost, delay, and access limitations of conventional logistics methods.

Span is being developed around that problem set.

Target missions

  • industrial lifting and logistics
  • temporary crane-replacement work
  • cargo movement over rugged terrain
  • emergency resupply
  • disaster response support

Development status

The current Span flight-test article is currently in development.

Reach remains the company’s lead commercial platform, while Span represents the larger future capability path.
